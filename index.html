Tart Crust

  • Time: 20min
  • Makes one crust
Quantity Ingredients
1 1/2 Cup AP Flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp. granulated sugar
1 1/2 stick very cold butter
1/4 Cup ice cold water

Step 1: Combine flour, salt, and sugar in a medium sized bowl.

Step 2: Add the butter and blend. Keep some ~1/2" chunks.

Step 3: Add the water. Lightly mix.

Step 4: Put dough on floured surface and knead a couple times to make a ball.

Source: Once Upon a Chef


Morton's Steakhouse Creamed Spinach Recipe

  • Time: 35min
  • Serves: 8
Quantity Ingredients
4 Tbsp. unsalted butter
1 medium onion, minced
3 cloves minced garlic
4 tsp. kosher salt, divided
1 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4 Cup AP flour
2 Cups half-and-half
2 1/4 pounds fresh spinach
4 oz. cream cheese

Step 1: Bring a large pot of water to boil with 2 tsp. salt.

Step 2: Add the spinach and cook until just wilted, about 1 minute.

Step 3: Drain the spinach.

Step 4: Rinse spinach and then squeeze the water out.

Step 5: Chop the spinach bundles into 3/4" width.

Step 6: In a large skillet melt the butter with the garlic and onions.

Step 7: Cook on medium low for 8-10 minutes, or until the onions are translucent.

Step 8: Add in the remaining kosher salt, black pepper, and nutmeg.

Step 9: Add in the flour and mix with a wooden spoon over low. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the aroma is a bit nutty.

Step 10: Add the half-and-half and cook until boiling..

Step 11: Add in the cream cheese.

Step 12: Cook for 6-8 minutes or until the mixture has thickened.

Step 13: Add in the spinach and stir to combine.

Source: Dinner Then Dessert

Microwave Chocolate Pudding

  • Time: 20min
  • Makes one crust
Quantity Ingredients
2 Cups milk
3 Tbsp. cornstarch
1/3 Cup granulated sugar
1/4 Cup cocoa powder
1.2 tsp. vanilla

Step 1: In a large microwave-safe container, mix cornstarch, sugar, and cocoa powder. Add milk, a little at a time and whisk together until smooth.

Step 2: Put in microwave, cook on medium power for 5 minutes. Take out and whisk mixture smooth.

Step 3: Place pudding back in microwave, cook another 4 minutes, then take out and whisk as before.

Step 4: Stir in vanilla.
